Welcome to The Shoestring Golfer!

Welcome to The Shoestring Golfer! My site is all about following the passion for golf……..wherever it takes you. And when you get there, trying to find the most affordable round of golf available!

Background to my Golfing Journey!

Over the last 13 years I have been fortunate enough to travel and work all over the World. My site is a living, breathing record of this, with stories, pictures and videos of some of the courses I have played, visited and worked at.

Thanks for visiting my site, I hope you enjoy it.

My clubs: I bought most of my equipment while working at the Polo Club of Boca Raton, Florida, at the end of 2005 / beginning of 2006. First I bought the Taylormade R5 10.5 degree Driver(with Aldila NVS 65-R shaft), followed by a real bargain on the Titleist 704CB Forged Irons (on True Temper R300 shafts), I then swopped sand wedges with my friend, Andre, to get a Titleist DCI 56 degree. The lob wedge, Titleist 6000S 60 degree, and putter, Ray Cook Blue Goose, I rescued out of an old bag when one of the members was getting rid of some old clubs! Thanks to Tony, the Caddy Master at Glen Oaks Club, New York, I scored a Ogio Stand Bag, which is starting to wear out, so Tony, if you are reading this…….! I normally wear a Footjoy WeatherSoft Glove and play with any of the hundreds of balls I found while clearing the driving range or cleaning golf carts at Glen Oaks.
